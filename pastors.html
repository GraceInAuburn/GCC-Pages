JEAN MILLIKEN

MINISTRY TEAM PASTOR

Jean is our Ministry Team Pastor. She has a passion to make fully devoted disciples of Jesus Christ. She provides administration for our staff team, leadership development, and mobilization through communication and implementation of the strategic plans and vision of the Senior Pastor and Elders. Jean is also on the Teaching Team at Grace. Her hobbies include: attending Broadway productions, gardening, and reading. She enjoys camping, fishing, and hunting with her husband, Dan, and spending time with her extended family that live in the Pacific NW. You can follow Jean's blog at http://soulthirsty.wordpress.com/

PAUL WARD

WORSHIP & ARTS PASTOR

Paul Ward is our Worship and Arts Pastor and has over 17 years of ministry experience leading people into the presence of God. He graduated from Yale in 1996. He has furthered his education at Calvin Theological Seminary and is nearing completion of an M.A. degree in Ministry Leadership from George Fox Evangelical Seminary. Paul and his wife, Kristen, have been married since 1996, and have three children: Emily, Ryan, and Amanda.
