Tom Bingham

Tom and his wife, Kay, met in high school, put their trust in Jesus as their Savior during college, and were married soon after. They first joined Grace as "young marrieds." Shortly after, Tom and Kay joined the pioneering effort to plant a church in Kent and served there for many years. They now serve at Grace in small group leadership, ESL, and International Partners ministries. Tom holds a Master's Degree in Applied Mathematics and works at Boeing. He has a passion for building multi-church cooperatives that support our International Partners. He seeks to align short-term missions with long-term goals.

David Kaneshiro

David Kaneshiro and his wife, Melanie, have been married since 1997 and have two adult children. They have been attending Grace since 2007, where they have served as small group leaders. David became a Christian in the early '80's and has been involved with International Student ministries in various capacities since that time. He is employed at World Vision. David's passions include transformational prayer and the mission of disciples making disciples.

Brad Kline

Brad has attended Grace since moving to Auburn in 2005. He has been married to JoAn since 1986. They have four children. He first came to faith in Jesus when he was a child living in Boise Idaho. He is a graduate of Northwest University with a degree in pastoral ministry. He is excited to be serving the church as an Elder in times such as these. He believes the greatest days are ahead of us as we prayerfully seek God and are obedient to fulfilling The Great Commission by making disciples for Jesus.

Mark Pampe

Mark and his wife, Robbie, met in high school in Wyoming and have been married since 1983. They moved to Washington in 1985 and have been attending Grace since 1997. They have two adult sons and one cute dog. Mark is an engineering manager at Boeing, Robbie is a real estate agent with Windermere, and together they lead the Marriage Investors ministry at Grace and share a passion for seeing marriages grow and flourish. For fun, they enjoy projects around the house or in Mark's workshop, relaxing in the back yard, and driving cars at the track. They live in the Lake Tapps area and host a small group in their home.

Dave Shirley

Dave and his wife, Malinda, have found Grace to be a wonderful place to raise their family. They have both enjoyed giving back through various teaching ministries since the late 80's. Dave's passion is studying God's Word and sharing it through Precept Ministries' inductive studies and in small groups. He also loves engaging in Christian apologetics. When not spending time with family or church, Dave can often be found having fun at Boeing.

Barry Wilber

Barry and his wife, Patty, greatly enjoy the opportunity to serve together in ministry. Currently they are leading Second Sunday Prison Ministry and Unshakable Parents, a support group for parents who have children battling addictions. Barry and Patty are also very active in Worship and Arts ministry and have formerly served as Marriage Mentors, taught blended family classes, and served in the High School ministry. They live in Auburn and have four children and three grandchildren. Barry and Patty own an executive recruiting business and work to enable client companies to raise the bar and position candidates to reach new horizons.
